Mission Focus

◎ Our Mission

To promote dance as a form of artistic expression, wellness, and community building while providing professional opportunities for Filipino dance artists.

⦿ Our Vision

To be the premier dance cooperative in the Philippines, recognized for excellence in instruction, innovation in corporate wellness, and preservation of dance culture.
